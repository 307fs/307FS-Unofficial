About the 307th (Unofficial)

The 307th Fighter Squadron ("Stingers") has a proud history within the Air Force Reserve, associated with F-15E Strike Eagle training and operations. This page offers publicly available, high-level information only and does not publish schedules, tail numbers linked to individuals, or sensitive procedures.
